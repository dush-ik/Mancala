Mancala

Instructions:

  • Once the player is chosen, they can select any pit from their side to play.
  • After the pit selection, all the numbers from that pit will be used to fill the other pits including player's own score pit while moving counter-clockwise.
  • The chosen player will keep playing in this fashion until their last pit has digit "1". If it happens then, other player will get the chance to choose a pit and play.
  • Once either of the players' entire pits get empty, then whoever has the most number in their score pit will be the winner.
